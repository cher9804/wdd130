Colombia

Colombia is a diverse and vibrant country in South America, known for its rich culture, music like vallenato and cumbia, stunning landscapes ranging from Caribbean beaches to the Andes mountains, and its delicious cuisine, including arepas, bandeja paisa, and world-class coffee. It also stands out for its history, warm people, and exceptional biodiversity.
